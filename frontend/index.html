How It Works

Three steps to cast your vote

Simple, secure, and takes under a minute. Your identity is verified before you vote.

01
🪪
Enter Your Enrollment
Type your unique enrollment number assigned during voter registration. This links your identity to the voting session.
02
📸
Face Verification
Your face is captured via webcam and matched against your registered biometric profile using MediaPipe AI.
03
🗳️
Select & Submit
Choose your candidate and submit. Your vote is written to the Ethereum blockchain as a tamper-proof transaction.
Why Vote Rakshak

Built for trust, security & transparency

Every layer of the system is designed to prevent fraud and protect voter privacy.

⛓️
Ethereum Smart Contracts
Votes are stored using Solidity contracts deployed via Truffle — no central authority can alter them.
🧠
AI Face Recognition
MediaPipe FaceMesh with 468 landmark points ensures only the registered voter is present at voting time.
🔐
JWT Admin Auth
Admin portal protected by token-based authentication — all admin actions are logged and auditable.
📡
Decentralised Ledger
Votes propagate across the Ethereum network via MetaMask and Ganache — no single point of failure.
Blockchain

Every vote is a block on the chain

Once submitted, your vote is hashed and appended to an ever-growing, immutable chain of records. Nobody — not even the admin — can change it.
